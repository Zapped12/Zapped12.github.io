Sullivan's Robot:
Sullivan's 2025 robot is the first robot made by the Five Star Robotics team, as Sullivan Heights has started participating in FIRST Robotics for the first time in over a decade, with the help of funding from sources such as fundraisers. Sullivan's robot was named Five Star, which participated in the 2025 FIRST Robotics competition in January/February, where it participated very well. Sullivan's Five Star robot will soon be developed again to participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition for January/February of 2026.
